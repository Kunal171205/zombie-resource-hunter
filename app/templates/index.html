What is a Zombie Resource?

Idle EC2: Instances with <5% CPU usage over a week. These are often "orphaned" servers from old projects or abandoned tests.

Available EBS: Storage volumes that aren't attached to any server. You pay for them every second they exist, even if they're empty!

Cost Optimization Tips

🚀 Upgrade to gp3: Save up to 20% compared to gp2 volumes with better performance.

⏰ Schedule Stops: Use "Instance Scheduler" to turn off dev boxes during weekends.

📏 Right-sizing: Move oversized instances to smaller families (e.g., from t3.large to t3.medium).
